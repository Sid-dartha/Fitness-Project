Tuesday - Back and Biceps (Age: 36-60)

Workout Details

  • Lat Pulldown: 3 sets x 10-12 reps
  • Seated Cable Row: 3 sets x 10-12 reps
  • Bent-over Dumbbell Rows: 3 sets x 10-12 reps
  • Barbell Bicep Curl: 3 sets x 10-12 reps
  • Hammer Curl: 3 sets x 12-15 reps
  • Cool-down: 5-10 minutes of light stretches for back and biceps

Precautions

  • Use controlled movements to reduce the risk of injury
  • Avoid lifting excessively heavy weights
  • Maintain a straight back during rowing movements
  • Focus on engaging the target muscles rather than using momentum
  • Stop if you feel pain or discomfort beyond normal muscle fatigue

Health Details

  • Workout duration: 45-60 minutes including rest periods
  • Target heart rate: 60-75% of max during exercises
  • Focus on maintaining flexibility with regular stretching
  • Calories burned: 300-500 depending on intensity
  • Improves posture and reduces risk of age-related back issues
