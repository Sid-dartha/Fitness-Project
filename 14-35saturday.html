Saturday - HIIT or Cardio (Age: 14-35)

Workout Details

  • Warm-up: 5 minutes of light jogging or dynamic stretches
  • High Knees: 30 seconds, rest 15 seconds (repeat 4 rounds)
  • Jump Squats: 30 seconds, rest 15 seconds (repeat 4 rounds)
  • Burpees: 20 seconds, rest 20 seconds (repeat 3 rounds)
  • Mountain Climbers: 30 seconds, rest 15 seconds (repeat 4 rounds)
  • Cool down: 5-10 minutes of light stretching

Precautions

  • Start at a comfortable pace if you're new to HIIT
  • Avoid jumping exercises if you have knee or joint pain
  • Stay hydrated throughout the session
  • Wear proper shoes to absorb impact during jumps
  • Focus on controlled breathing during high-intensity intervals

Food Guidelines

  • Eat a small snack with carbs and protein 30-60 minutes before the workout
  • Stay hydrated with water or an electrolyte drink
  • Post-workout: Consume a balanced meal with protein and healthy carbs
  • Avoid heavy or greasy foods before HIIT sessions
  • For younger participants, include fruits like bananas or apples

Health Details

  • Target heart rate: 70-85% of max during high-intensity intervals
  • Rest periods between exercises should be active (light movement)
  • Calories burned: 500-700 depending on intensity
  • Workout duration: 20-30 minutes, excluding warm-up and cool-down
  • Helps improve cardiovascular endurance and metabolism
