Friday - Full Body Strength Training (Age: 14-35)

Workout Details

  • Squats (Bodyweight or Weighted): 4 sets x 10-12 reps
  • Push-Ups (Knee variation if needed): 3 sets x 10-15 reps
  • Deadlifts (Dumbbells for beginners): 4 sets x 8-10 reps
  • Overhead Press: 3 sets x 10-12 reps
  • Lunges (Bodyweight or Weighted): 3 sets x 12 reps per leg
  • Plank Hold: 3 sets x 30-60 seconds

Precautions

  • Focus on proper form, especially for squats and deadlifts
  • Warm up with dynamic stretches to avoid injury
  • Adjust weights according to your strength level
  • Ensure core engagement during all exercises
  • Rest adequately between sets, especially during compound lifts

Food Guidelines

  • Include lean proteins like chicken, eggs, or legumes post-workout
  • Eat a balanced meal with complex carbs, proteins, and healthy fats
  • Hydrate with water or electrolyte drinks
  • Younger individuals should consume snacks like yogurt or fruits
  • Avoid processed foods and excessive sugar

Health Details

  • Target heart rate: 60-75% of max for moderate intensity
  • Calories burned: 500-700 depending on intensity
  • Focus on full-body engagement and controlled breathing
  • Total workout duration: 50-70 minutes
  • Incorporate cooldown stretches post-workout
