Wednesday - Legs and Shoulders (Age: 14-35)

Workout Details

  • Squats (Bodyweight for beginners): 4 sets x 10-15 reps
  • Leg Press: 3 sets x 10-12 reps
  • Deadlifts (Focus on form): 4 sets x 8-10 reps
  • Lunges: 3 sets x 10 reps per leg
  • Overhead Press: 4 sets x 8-12 reps
  • Arnold Press: 3 sets x 10-12 reps
  • Lateral Raises: 3 sets x 12-15 reps

Precautions

  • Warm up for at least 10 minutes to prevent knee and back strain
  • Focus on proper depth during squats, avoid overloading
  • Keep your core engaged during overhead presses
  • Avoid jerky movements in lateral raises
  • Take breaks if you feel fatigued; avoid overtraining

Food Guidelines

  • Consume a balanced meal with carbs, protein, and healthy fats
  • Post-workout: Include protein-rich options like Greek yogurt or a smoothie

  • For younger individuals, include calcium-rich foods like milk or cheese
  • Avoid processed and sugary snacks

Health Details

  • Target heart rate: 60-75% of max for effective training
  • Rest 60-90 seconds between sets
  • Calories burned: 500-700 (varies by intensity)
  • Focus on building lower-body and shoulder strength
  • Total workout duration: 60-75 minutes
