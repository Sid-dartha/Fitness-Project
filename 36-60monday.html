Monday - Chest and Triceps (Age: 36-60)

Workout Details

  • Incline Push-Ups: 3 sets x 8-10 reps
  • Flat Bench Press (lightweight): 3 sets x 8-12 reps
  • Chest Flyes (low resistance): 3 sets x 12-15 reps
  • Tricep Dips (bench-supported): 3 sets x 10-12 reps
  • Overhead Dumbbell Tricep Extensions: 3 sets x 8-10 reps
  • Wall Push-Ups: 2 sets x 12-15 reps

Precautions

  • Warm up thoroughly for 5-10 minutes before starting
  • Use controlled, slow movements to avoid injury
  • Focus on proper posture and alignment
  • Stop immediately if you feel sharp pain or discomfort
  • Avoid lifting heavy weights; prioritize form over intensity

Health Details

  • Target heart rate: 60-75% of max for safe exertion
  • Rest period: 1-2 minutes between sets
  • Workout duration: 30-45 minutes
  • Focus on joint-friendly exercises to protect shoulders and elbows
  • Calories burned: Approx. 200-300 depending on intensity
