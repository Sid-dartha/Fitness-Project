Friday - Full Body Strength Training (Age: 36-60)

Workout Details

  • Bodyweight Squats: 3 sets x 10-12 reps
  • Push-Ups (on knees if needed): 3 sets x 8-10 reps
  • Bent-Over Rows (with light weights): 3 sets x 12 reps
  • Glute Bridges: 3 sets x 15 reps
  • Overhead Dumbbell Press: 3 sets x 10-12 reps
  • Plank Hold: 3 rounds of 20-30 seconds

Precautions

  • Warm up for 5-10 minutes before starting strength exercises
  • Maintain proper posture during exercises to avoid injury
  • Use light weights to avoid strain, especially for beginners
  • Stop immediately if you feel discomfort or pain

Health Details

  • Improves overall muscle strength and stability
  • Helps enhance bone density and reduce risk of osteoporosis
  • Calories burned: 200-400 depending on intensity
  • Workout duration: 30-45 minutes, including warm-up and cool-down
  • Encourages better joint mobility and functional fitness
