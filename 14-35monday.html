Monday - Chest and Triceps

Workout Details

  • Bench Press: 4 sets x 8-12 reps
  • Incline Dumbbell Press: 3 sets x 10-12 reps
  • Chest Flyes: 3 sets x 12-15 reps
  • Tricep Pushdowns: 4 sets x 12-15 reps
  • Overhead Tricep Extensions: 3 sets x 12-15 reps
  • Diamond Push-ups: 3 sets to failure

Precautions

  • Always warm up properly before starting chest exercises
  • Maintain proper form throughout movements
  • Keep shoulders back and chest up during exercises
  • Don't lock out elbows during tricep exercises
  • If you feel any shoulder pain, stop immediately

Food Guidelines

  • Consume protein-rich foods (25-30g) within 2 hours of workout
  • Stay hydrated - drink water before, during, and after workout
  • Include complex carbs for energy
  • Avoid heavy meals 2-3 hours before workout
  • Consider BCAAs during workout for muscle preservation

Health Details

  • Target heart rate should be 65-85% of max
  • Rest 60-90 seconds between sets
  • Expected calories burned: 400-600
  • Focus on mind-muscle connection
  • Total workout duration: 45-60 minutes
