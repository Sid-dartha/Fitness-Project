Thursday - Rest or Light Cardio (Age: 14-35)

Workout Details

  • Engage in light cardio like walking, cycling, or yoga for 20-30 minutes
  • Focus on mobility exercises and stretching to improve flexibility
  • Practice deep breathing to relax the body and mind
  • Optional: Light bodyweight exercises like planks or wall sits
  • Use foam rollers to relieve muscle tension
  • Prioritize recovery and low-intensity movement

Precautions

  • Avoid overexertion; keep movements light and gentle
  • Stay hydrated, especially if engaging in light cardio
  • If muscles feel sore, prioritize rest and recovery
  • Use proper posture during mobility exercises
  • Stop if you feel any discomfort or dizziness

Food Guidelines

  • Focus on balanced meals with protein, complex carbs, and healthy fats
  • Snack on fruits or nuts for sustained energy
  • Stay hydrated throughout the day
  • For younger individuals, include calcium-rich foods like yogurt or milk
  • Avoid heavy, greasy foods that can slow recovery

Health Details

  • Recovery is essential for muscle repair and growth
  • Light cardio can help improve circulation and reduce soreness
  • Calorie burn: 200-300 (depending on activity level)
  • Focus on mental relaxation and reducing stress
  • Total duration: 20-30 minutes of light activity
