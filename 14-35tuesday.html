Tuesday - Back and Biceps (Age: 14-35)

Workout Details

  • Pull-ups (Assisted if necessary): 3 sets x 6-10 reps
  • Barbell Rows (Start light): 4 sets x 8-10 reps
  • Lat Pulldowns: 3 sets x 10-12 reps
  • Dumbbell Deadlifts: 3 sets x 10 reps (ensure proper form)
  • Bicep Curls (Focus on controlled motion): 3 sets x 12-15 reps
  • Hammer Curls: 2 sets x 12-15 reps

Precautions

  • Use weights that are manageable, focusing on proper form
  • Ensure back is straight during rows to avoid injury
  • Warm up adequately, especially for younger participants
  • Don’t lift too heavy too soon; build gradually
  • Stop if you experience any discomfort or strain

Food Guidelines

  • Include lean proteins like eggs, chicken, or tofu post-workout
  • Stay hydrated—drink at least 2-3 liters of water daily
  • For younger participants, include calcium-rich snacks
  • Avoid sugary drinks; opt for natural juices or coconut water
  • Include healthy fats like avocado or nuts

Health Details

  • Target heart rate: 60-75% of max for effective fat burn
  • Rest 60-90 seconds between sets
  • Calories burned: 400-600 (varies by intensity)
  • Focus on building strength and endurance gradually
  • Total workout duration: 45-60 minutes
