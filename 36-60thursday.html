Thursday - Rest or Light Cardio (Age: 36-60)

Workout Details

  • Rest day: Focus on active recovery with light stretches or yoga
  • Optional light cardio: 20-30 minutes of walking or cycling at an easy pace
  • Practice deep breathing exercises to relax
  • Engage in activities that promote mobility, such as tai chi or pilates

Precautions

  • Listen to your body and avoid overexertion
  • If doing light cardio, maintain a low heart rate (50-60% of max)
  • Avoid strenuous activities to allow for proper recovery
  • Wear comfortable clothing and shoes for light cardio
  • Stay hydrated and avoid prolonged inactivity

Health Details

  • Encourages muscle recovery and reduces fatigue
  • Helps maintain flexibility and joint mobility
  • Improves blood circulation without taxing the body
  • Promotes mental relaxation and reduces stress
  • Total activity duration: 20-40 minutes, as per comfort
