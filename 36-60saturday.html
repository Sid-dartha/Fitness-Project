Saturday - HIIT or Cardio (Age: 36-60)

Workout Details

  • Warm-up: 5-10 minutes of walking or light cycling
  • Low-impact Jumping Jacks: 30 seconds, rest 20 seconds (repeat 3 rounds)
  • Modified Burpees: 20 seconds, rest 20 seconds (repeat 3 rounds)
  • Step-ups (using a sturdy surface): 30 seconds per leg, rest 20 seconds
  • Walking Lunges: 30 seconds, rest 20 seconds (repeat 3 rounds)
  • Cool down: 5-10 minutes of light stretching or yoga poses

Precautions

  • Avoid high-impact exercises if you have joint issues
  • Focus on controlled, slow movements
  • Take longer rest periods if needed
  • Monitor your heart rate and stay within a comfortable range
  • Stop immediately if you feel dizzy or unwell

Health Details

  • Target heart rate: 60-75% of max for safety
  • Workout duration: 20-30 minutes, excluding warm-up and cool-down
  • Calories burned: 300-500, depending on intensity
  • Helps maintain cardiovascular health and weight management
  • Focus on maintaining proper posture throughout
