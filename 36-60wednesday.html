Wednesday - Legs and Shoulders (Age: 36-60)

Workout Details

  • Bodyweight Squats: 3 sets x 10-12 reps
  • Leg Press (low weight): 3 sets x 10-12 reps
  • Calf Raises: 3 sets x 15-20 reps
  • Dumbbell Shoulder Press: 3 sets x 10-12 reps
  • Side Lateral Raises: 3 sets x 12-15 reps
  • Front Dumbbell Raises: 3 sets x 12-15 reps
  • Optional: 10-minute light cardio cool-down

Precautions

  • Start with low weights to avoid strain on joints
  • Maintain a straight back during squats and presses
  • Avoid sudden jerky movements during shoulder exercises
  • Ensure proper warm-up to prevent stiffness or injuries
  • Stop if you feel sharp pain during any movement

Health Details

  • Target heart rate: 60-75% of max
  • Focus on proper form rather than heavy weights
  • Rest 60-90 seconds between sets
  • Total workout duration: 45-60 minutes
  • Improves joint flexibility and muscle endurance
